# DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
- Corporate industrial website for a steel supplier.
- Mood: disciplined, reliable, production-oriented, not startup-like.
- Overall impression: a Vietnamese manufacturing brand with clear hierarchy, restrained polish, and strong operational credibility.
- Avoid playful gradients, soft SaaS cards, and generic AI landing page aesthetics.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- Brand Green: `#0E5A3F`
  Primary brand surface, headers, nav emphasis, section anchors.
- Deep Green: `#093927`
  Footer, hero overlay, high-contrast dark areas.
- Accent Red: `#C73A2C`
  Primary CTA, highlights, small emphasis lines, active chips.
- Mist Background: `#EDF2EA`
  Page background for light sections.
- White Surface: `#FFFFFF`
  Product blocks, contact rows, content panels.
- Body Text: `#163725`
  Main readable text.
- Muted Text: `#5D6E63`
  Supporting copy and lower-priority details.

## 3. Typography Rules
- Headings: condensed, assertive, uppercase-friendly.
- Body: clean Vietnamese-first sans serif.
- Headlines should feel industrial and executive, not editorial or playful.
- Keep line lengths controlled and avoid decorative typography.

## 4. Component Stylings
- Buttons: square-to-soft radius, bold uppercase labels, solid red primary CTA.
- Navigation: clean horizontal desktop nav, light corporate mobile panel.
- Panels: light surfaces with subtle shadow, not floating glassmorphism.
- Hero band and stats: strong information blocks with clear separation and minimal ornament.

## 5. Layout Principles
- Use full-width section bands with constrained inner content.
- Keep the first viewport decisive: brand, operational promise, contact path.
- Organize sections around business tasks: supply capability, product demand, process, contact.
- Prefer fewer sections with clearer jobs over many decorative blocks.

## 6. Depth & Elevation
- Depth should come from section contrast, border rhythm, and limited shadow.
- Use heavier contrast in hero and footer only.
- Avoid layered card-on-card compositions.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts
- Do make the page feel like a real steel supply company.
- Do keep product and delivery language practical.
- Do use red sparingly as an action color.
- Do not mention repo, deploy, source code, or internal implementation inside visible UI.
- Do not use orange or purple as dominant hues.
- Do not make the site feel like a startup template.

## 8. Responsive Behavior
- Collapse navigation cleanly into a compact mobile menu.
- Maintain strong text contrast over photography on mobile.
- Stack stats, cards, and contact items vertically on narrow screens.
- Preserve fixed, stable button sizing and avoid text overlap.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide
- Build with a Hoa Phat-like corporate palette: deep green primary, red CTA accent, pale green-gray canvas.
- Keep the steel industry tone operational, direct, and trustworthy.
- Favor real construction imagery, simple section bands, and disciplined spacing.
